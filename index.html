Words that I Learnt from the Story

Lingering:
Meaning: Something that lasts for a long time or continues to exist or stay beyond the usual or expected time.
Example: The guests were still lingering in the garden long after the party ended.

Rekindling:
Meaning: The act of reviving or bringing back something that has diminished, faded, or stopped, such as emotions, relationships, interests, or even a fire.
Example: Reading that old novel helped in rekindling her love for literature.

Passionate:
Meaning: It describes someone who has strong, intense feelings or enthusiasm about something. It can refer to deep emotions, fervour, or dedication towards a person, activity, or cause.
Example: She is a passionate photographer, always eager to capture the perfect shot.
